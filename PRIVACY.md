> **Draft — for attorney review.** This policy is written for a product whose users include

> 13–16). It is **not legal advice** and is **not yet in force.** The specific data items,

> policy for how personal information is handled.

## Plain-language summary (not the legal terms)

- We collect as little about you as we can.
- **If you're under 13 (or your country's minimum age), a parent or guardian has to say yes
  before we collect anything from you**, and they can see it or ask us to delete it anytime.
- We **never sell** your information, and we don't let other companies take it.
- We don't show you targeted ads, and we don't use your stuff to train AI.
- You (or your parent) can ask to see, fix, or delete your information.

The summary is for convenience only. The policy below controls.
