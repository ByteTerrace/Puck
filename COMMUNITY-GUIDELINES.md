ByteTerrace.Puck is meant to be a friendly, creative place where people — including kids and students —
build and share avatars, stages, and worlds. These rules keep it that way. They apply to
everything you upload, share, or do on the ByteTerrace.Puck hub, and they are part of the
[Platform Terms](PLATFORM-TERMS.md).

## The basics

1. **Be kind.** No harassment, bullying, threats, or targeting other people.
2. **No hate.** Nothing that attacks or demeans people based on who they are (race, ethnicity,
   religion, disability, sex, gender, sexual orientation, age, or similar).
3. **Keep it appropriate.** No sexual or explicit content, no graphic violence or gore, and
   nothing else inappropriate for a community that includes children.
4. **No dangerous or illegal content.** Nothing promoting self-harm, illegal activity, weapons
   instructions, drugs, or similar.
5. **Upload only your own work.** Don't upload content you don't have the rights to. This mirrors
   [Platform Terms](PLATFORM-TERMS.md) Section 10 — your submissions must be yours.
6. **No spam or scams.** No spam, advertising, phishing, malware, or attempts to trick or defraud
   other users.
7. **No cheating the system.** Don't exploit, hack, scrape, or abuse the hub, and don't try to
   get around its limits or protections.
8. **Don't impersonate** ByteTerrace, moderators, or other people.

## Protecting kids (child safety)

Because young people use ByteTerrace.Puck, we take safety seriously and have **zero tolerance** for:

- **Predatory or grooming behavior** toward minors, in any form.
- **Sexualization of minors** or any child sexual abuse material (CSAM) — this is reported to the
  relevant authorities.
- **Soliciting personal information** from other users (especially minors) — real names,
  addresses, schools, phone numbers, passwords — or trying to move conversations off-platform to
  contact a minor privately.
- **Sharing someone's private information** (doxxing).
